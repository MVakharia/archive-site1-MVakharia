Asteroid Assault - Top-down space shooter - 48 hour game jam

The product of a game jam I participated in back in November of 2020.

The goal: shoot your way through an asteroid belt. If too many asteroids hit you, you die.

Download Android .apk View Source code

Gameplay




Shoot and destroy the asteroids heading directly for your ship.

For extra points, you can shoot the ones that won't hit your ship.







The cursor on the screen, a small blue-green disc, tells you which way your laser gun is pointing.

To fire your laser, press and hold the screen to move the cursor towards your finger, then release to send a laser towards the cursor.





You'll see a few different kinds of asteroid on your journey.

The blue ones will temporarily disable your shield.

The turquoise-coloured ones will temporarily freeze it.

If your shield is frozen, the next asteroid that hits you will shatter it. Shields don't recharge once they've been depleted.




At the end of each round is a shop: stop here to repair your damaged shields or hull.


You can also purchase upgrades in the shop. They'll increase your hull's health, your shield's health, and lower the cooldown on your laser beam so that you can fire it more often.





At the end of every second level you'll find a hostile enemy spaceship.


Destroy the spaceship before it destroys yours, by shooting down the projectiles and then the ship.





About the game jam

The game jam was staggered over 4 days of 12 hours as opposed to a single 48 hour stint over 2 days, in an effort to promote healthier development conditions, flexibility, and better coding practices.
