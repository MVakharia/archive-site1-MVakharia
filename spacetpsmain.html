Untitled vertical third-person shooter (remake of university project, 2014) - TBD

An upcoming remake of a game developed during my final year at university.

Gameplay

A shooter with full freedom of movement: the directional keys move the player character relative to their current orientation, and mouse movement along the y-axis is not constrained to the 180 degrees between looking straight up and looking straight down: you can do as many backflips and as many front-flips as you like.

Aside from the above, gameplay is fairly simple: spawns point will appear at random locations, and enemies will spawn from them. Enemies will move towards you and melee attack you to death, so shoot them before they can kill you.

Original

The second of two games developed that year, using Unity, UnityScript (Unity's implementation of JavaScript), and Autodesk 3DS MAX.
