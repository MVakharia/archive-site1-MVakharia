Untitled endless driver/auto-driver (cancelled)

An auto/endless driving game I partially developed in December of last year.

Think Snake vs Block, but the snake is a car and the blocks are oncoming traffic.

Unfortunately, development of the game was cancelled, but thankfully I am able to share links to the game and the source code, which you can click below.

Download .apk (Android) Source code

Gameplay





Touch the left and right sides of the screen to steer.









Smash into the oncoming traffic that will do the least damage, indicated by the numbers above them.









Pick up repair orbs along the way to keep the car going.









If your car is destroyed, it's game over - that is, in this version of the game. The complete version would have allowed you to buy extra lives or watch an ad to get a new car.








Your statistics across all games are saved and may be viewed in the main menu.
